ABOUT

The Avengers are a team of superheroes that first appeared in American comic books published by Marvel Comics, created by writer-editor Stan Lee and artist/co-plotter Jack Kirby. Labeled "Earth's Mightiest Heroes", the Avengers originally consisted of Ant-Man, the Hulk, Iron Man, Thor, the Wasp, Captain America, and now the roster has expanded to include heroes such as Hawkeye, Black Widow, and Spiderman. As the heroes who have time and time again saved the world from adversity, the Avengers vow to fight "the foes no single superhero can withstand."
